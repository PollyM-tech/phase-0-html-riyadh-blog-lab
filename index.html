Travel

Once a mudbrick waystation along desert trading routes, Riyadh is now one of the wealthiest cities in the world.

History

Riyadh is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia. It is also the capital of Riyadh Province and belongs to the historical regions of Najd and Al-Yamama.

Sources

The city is divided into 15 municipal districts, managed by Riyadh Municipality headed by the mayor of Riyadh, and the Riyadh Development Authority, chaired by the governor of Riyadh Province.
